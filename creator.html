About the Team

This exhibition platform was conceptualized and developed by Debargha Sarkar, an experienced coder and website designer who led the entire design, development, and implementation process.

Sagnika Sinha contributed by providing accurate and detailed information regarding the names of the exhibition stalls, ensuring the data was well-organized and accessible.

Rupsa Kundu assisted similarly in compiling and verifying stall information, supporting the project with careful attention to detail.

The project was guided and provided by Dipan Bandyopadhyay, our mentor, who provided the concept and oversight.

This platform represents a collaborative effort, with Debargha Sarkar overseeing all aspects and integrating contributions from the team to create a cohesive, professional exhibition experience.
